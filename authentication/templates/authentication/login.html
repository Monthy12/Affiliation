NABPTEX AFFILIATION PORTAL

The National Board for Professional and Technician Examination (NABPTEX) is an agency of the Ministry of Education established by an Act of Parliament, Act 1994 (Act 492). The Board is responsible for the formulation and administration of schemes of examination, evaluation, assessment, certification and standard for skills competence and syllabus competence for non- university tertiary institutions, professional bodies and private institutions.

The Affiliation Process sets up the roadmap in qualifying an institution to be able to run NABPTEX Approved Higher National Diploma and Diploma programmes of study. Any tertiary institution that would like to be Affiliated to NABPTEX and run its programmes must first put in an application via this portal. To do so you need to click to sign up here, or login at the adjacent form if you have already created an account with us.

If you are new to the system then carefully read the instructions/information about the Affiliation process on the Instruction page. We are excited to have you join us.
